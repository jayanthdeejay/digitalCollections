Fair Use and Copyright

The Digital Collections & Repositories department encourages fair use of all digital content found on our websites and digital repositories. For assistance in evaluating what constitutes fair use, we recommend the Fair Use Evaluator from the American Library Association, Office of Information Technology Policy.

All content on this web site, except where otherwise noted, is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivatives 4.0 International License. Content, except where otherwise noted, may be downloaded and shared, provided that the intended use is for educational and research purposes, not commercial purposes, and provided that citations to the source are given. For further inquiries, see our Contact/Help page. Images in Luna collections may be downloaded using the Luna Export features.

If a higher resolution image is desired or if you have questions about any of our digital repositories (Digital Resource Commons, LUNA Image and Media Repository, and Scholar@UC) please contact our staff.

If you create presentations or other educational materials using content from this site, please share your content with us!

See also the Acceptable Use Policy for University of Cincinnati Libraries, and the Copyright Infringement Policy of the University of Cincinnati.
