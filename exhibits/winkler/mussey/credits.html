Web Site Contributions

  • Katherine T. Barkley, formerly Medical Librarian, Jewish Hospital, Cincinnati, Ohio, during the 1970s and 1980s, compiled a history of the Mussey family, which served as an important resource for this web site.
  • Carrie Hill-Harriss, a freelance website developer, composed, designed, and developed this website.
  • Christopher Hoolihan, Head of Rare Books and Manuscripts at the Edward G. Miner Library, University of Rochester Medical Center, served as consultant to the Mussey Collection in 2010. His report is cited numerous times in this web site.

 

Dr. and Mrs. Robert Auer Wolf 's gift to the Winkler Center made possible the development of this website to promote the unique Reuben Dimond Mussey Collection.

The Center is very grateful to Dr. and Mrs. Wolf for their generosity.
