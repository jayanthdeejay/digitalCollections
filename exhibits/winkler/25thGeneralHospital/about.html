About this Project

To honor the veterans of the 25th who served in World War II, and to keep the memory of their achievements alive in the minds of our younger generations, the Winkler Center presents:

  • An exhibit that is periodically on display at the Center and that may be borrowed for display in other locations
  • This web site presenting the 25th's history with a broad range of visual and textual resources
  • A program about the 25th, featuring a lecture by Dale C. Smith, PhD, medical and military historian at the Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences
  • Oral history interviews with veterans of the 25th
  • A digitized version of Dr. Jean Stevenson's Path to War,
    a documentary film about the 25th shot on location
