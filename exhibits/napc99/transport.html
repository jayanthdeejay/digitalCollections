FLYING
Cincinnati is served by all major airlines and is a Delta hub.  The Greater Cincinnati International Airport is in Northern Kentucky.  Services to downtown include taxi, shuttle and bus.
    Typical taxi fare: $22.00
    Jet'Port express shuttle: $12.00 (one way) $16.00 (round-trip)
