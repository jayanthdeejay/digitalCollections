Willis G. Meyer Map Collection

The Willis G. Meyer Map Collection contains digitized maps from a print collection of over 130,000 maps. The maps chosen for digitization and georectification fall within three categories: produced by the Department of Geology at the University of Cincinnati, a history of heavy use on-site at the library, and of research interest to the graduate students working on this project.

Neil A. Armstrong Commemorative Archive

The Neil A. Armstrong Commemorative Archive contains images, documents and texts, primarily from the University Archives, relating to the teaching career of Neil Armstrong at the University of Cincinnati (1971-1979).
