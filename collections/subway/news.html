  • ‘Lost in Transit’ -- Cincinnati Magazine May 2013

    The May 2013 edition of Cincinnati Magazine features a photo essay describing the story of the never-completed Cincinnati Subway, and the joint digitization project undertaken by University of Cincinnati’s Archives & Rare Books Library and Digital Collections department to convert the print and negative collection to an online repository. A digital gallery of select images is available in the Cincinnati Magazine online gallery, ‘A Land Down Under’.
