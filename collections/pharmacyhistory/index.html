Winkler Center History of Pharmacy

This digital collection consist of table lists containing the names of professional doctorate (PharmD) Degree Alumni from the University of Cincinnati, College of Pharmacy. It was initially published in Pharmaceutical Education in the Queen City: 150 Years of Service, 1850-2000 by Michael A. Flannery and Dennis B. Worthen, New York: Pharmaceutical Products Press, 2001.

Also seen in this collection are scanned Kodak color slides donated to the Winkler Center by Dr. J. Richard Wuest, Professor Emeritus of the University of Cincinnati, College of Pharmacy. The original images were all taken in April 1974 at the old Cincinnati College of Pharmacy building, just prior to the building's demolition.
