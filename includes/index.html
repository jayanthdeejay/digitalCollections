About the Digital Resource Commons

The Ohio Digital Resource Commons (DRC) is an initiative of OhioLINK and its member libraries, to collect, preserve, and distribute digital material. The Ohio Digital Resource Commons is a network of repositories that enables institutions to save, discover and share the instructional, research, historic and creative materials produced by the University System of Ohio and Ohio's liberal arts colleges.

About UC Libraries Digital Collections

The mission of the UC Libraries' digital collections initiative is to create and provide access to digital materials in support of the Libraries mission to provide excellent research collections and expedite access to knowledge. These efforts further the University of Cincinnati's comprehensive mission of teaching, learning, research and community service.

What's New

New Site: The Oesper Collections in the History of Chemistry


This new site highlights The Oesper Collections in the History of Chemistry, a joint venture of the Ralph E. Oesper Chemistry-Biology Library and the Department of Chemistry of the University of Cincinnati. Virtual Tours are available for the Museum and the circa 1900 Lab. From the Museum virtual tour, or from the Museum Displays, users may click on a case, then a shelf and then an individual apparatus for 3-D and high resolution images of each apparatus.

New Collection: The Cincinnatian (The official University of Cincinnati yearbook)

The Cincinnatian was published annually by the students of the University of Cincinnati from 1894 through 1972, except 1906, and sporadically since 1972. It offers primary material on students, faculty, the campus, events and organizations, as well as the relationship of the university to the community and student reaction to national events.
